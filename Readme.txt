How to play!


WASD 		-> to move
Spacebar	-> Jump
Left click 	-> to fire a projectile.

Projectiles fire 45degrees downwards. Enemy Projectiles move directly to last known player location on sight. Projectiles can also activate ONOFF blocks.


Objective:
Get to the end of the world. Don't get hit by the clones, but make sure you also watch out for the ONOFF blocks.
